excerpt: 'In this paper we introduce an improved surface subdivision rule for a 4-point approximating curve subdivision. The novel 4-point approximating surface is very similar to the Doo-Sabin scheme and it extends the representation ability of the Doo-Sabin scheme with a tension parameter, by adjusting which the subdivision surface could be modified. Then we add area control to the new scheme by numerical method, which considerably improves the control of surfaces.'
