excerpt: 'Smart mobile phones have become the most popular electronic equipment; many operations have been transferred from computers to mobiles. We achieved an image interpolation algorithm on android platform. This algorithm could generate high-resolution images from low-resolution images by the pixel-level data-dependent triangulation and a triangular interpolation. The algorithm we achieved on android is more lightweight and the result image is sharper.'
